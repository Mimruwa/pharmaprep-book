Overview

What is PharmaPrep?

PharmaPrep: Pharmacology Exam Guide is a digital pharmacology study guide created to support exam preparation and organized revision. It is made for learners who need a clean, structured, and professional study resource.

The goal of PharmaPrep is to make pharmacology review easier by presenting the learning experience in a focused digital format. It is suitable for pharmacy students, pharmacists, and healthcare learners who want reliable study organization.

This eBook is designed as an educational revision resource. It is not medical advice, treatment advice, or a replacement for official textbooks, clinical guidelines, or professional training.
